This is not a instructions on how to loot.

This is a direct guide on where to sell the loot that you got from loot diving.

Elster's Cave

Elster's Cave is just to the left of Sardon's Tower. Elster is a hoarder, so his cave is a mess. Elster can buy all your loot for a lump sum or individual items one by one. He accepts anything, but you'll get fewer gralats than from other NPCs.

NPC: Elster the Hoarder

Items Bought:
All loot items
Price: Low fixed rate (e.g. Cracked Shield = 13g, Gold Doubloon = 50g)

Pyrat Tent

He is standing on a small tent in York Town. It should be on the south of the horse track.

NPC: Jack the Pyrat

Items Bought:
Gold Doubloon – 75g
Silver Doubloon – 75g
Heavy Anchor – 75g
Ship Helm – 75g

Lantern Stand

He is located between Onnet Town and Snow Town, in a small stall where he sells the lanterns.

NPC: Lantern Seller

Items Bought:
Old Lantern – 18g

Taylor Richards' House

In the south of Graal City before you enter the city itself (near the river bank).

NPC: Taylor Richards

AItems Bought:
Ruined Scarf – 18g
Old Underwear – 18g

Owl's Nest

Located in the cave out side the Destiny's Tower. Coin Toss is required to enter Owl's Nest.

NPC: Owl's Nest Leader

Items Bought:
Gold Necklace – 150g
Gold Nugget – 150g
Gold Pearl – 150g
Water Amulet – 150g
Magical Crystal – 150g
Ornate Ring – 150g

Horse Stable

The stable is located in the Woods of Master Li almost next to his dojo.

NPC: Earl, Stable Hand

Items Bought:
Old Horseshoe – 18g

Oasis Island

Located in the VIP Area, you can travel using the boat located in Delta Island.

NPC: Joshua the Baby

Items Bought:
Conch Shell – 18g
Scallop Shell – 18g
Other seashell loot – 18g

Mysterious Cave

Hidden in a side cave inside the Rail Cave tunnel near Snow Town.

NPC: Old Geezer

Items Bought:
Odd-Shaped Gem – 18g

Snow Town Blacksmith

Located in Snow Town. A draisine or lantern is required to enter the town.

NPC: Blacksmith

Items Bought:
Broken Sword – 18g
Chainless Flail – 18g
Cracked Shield – 18g
Defuse Bomb – 18g
Dull Arrow – 18g

Swamp Town

Near Miser’s House, in the Swamp Town region. Locate the Green Blob cave at west part of the town.

NPC: Floydian

Items Bought:
Fishing Lure – 18g
Old Fishing Hook – 18g

Black Orchid Club

Inside Black Orchid Club, MoD Town

NPC: Raphael

Items Bought:
Dirty Mug – 18g

Railroad Station Gift Shop

Inside Graal City, in the Train Station gift shop.

NPC: Railroad Gift Shop NPC

Items Bought:
