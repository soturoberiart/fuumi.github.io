Elster's Cave

Located left of Sardon’s Tower. Elster is a messy hoarder who buys all loot — either all at once or one by one — but pays lower than others.

NPC: Elster the Hoarder

Items Bought: All loot items
Price: Low fixed rate (e.g. Cracked Shield = 13g, Gold Doubloon = 50g)
