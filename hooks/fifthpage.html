This is a direct guide on where to sell the loot that you got from loot diving.

Elster's Cave

Elster's Cave is to the left of Sardon's Tower. Elster buys loot either all at once or per item. He accepts anything but gives lower rates.

NPC: Elster the Hoarder

Items Bought: All loot items
Price: Low fixed rate (e.g. Cracked Shield = 13g, Gold Doubloon = 50g)
